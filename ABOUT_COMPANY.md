# About the Company

We help accommodations connect their guests with trusted local hospitality partners.

The product is built for a simple guest moment: someone arrives at a stay, scans a QR code, and immediately sees nearby places, experiences, and offers selected for that specific accommodation. There is no app to install, no guest account to create, and no login wall between arrival and discovery.

For accommodation owners, the service turns a stay into a more useful local guide. Guests get a curated list of restaurants, cafes, bars, tours, spas, activities, and other local experiences close to where they are staying.

For hospitality owners, the service creates a direct path to nearby travelers who are already in the area and ready to visit. Partner venues can offer discounts, welcome drinks, set menus, upgrades, or other benefits that make the guest's choice easier.

The company sits between both sides. It curates useful partnerships, keeps the guest experience simple, and helps local businesses turn nearby stays into real visits.

## What We Value

- Low-friction guest discovery
- Local recommendations over generic listings
- Useful partnerships for both accommodations and venues
- Clear, honest offers that staff can honor in person
- A simple product that works from the moment a guest arrives
